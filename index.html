What is the central angle of a polygon?
The central angle of a polygon is the angle whose vertex is the center and whose rays or sides contain the end points of a side of the regular polygon.
How to calculate central angle of a polygon?
To calculate central angle of a polygon you should use the following formula:
central angle = (360 / n) degrees
where:
n = the number of sides
